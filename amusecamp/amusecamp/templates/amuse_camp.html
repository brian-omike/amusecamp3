About Amuse Camp

We pride in providing a great way to keep your children actively engaged through exercise, fun obstacle course challenges, different themed nature scavenger hunts, socialising and working together in a very diverse group of other kids, learning and above all experiencing fun through both structured and non structured activities the way kids love.All you've gotta do is sign them up, drop them at 9am and pick them up at 12pm. We will have made great use of the time and their energy.Get in touch with us if you have any queries.
